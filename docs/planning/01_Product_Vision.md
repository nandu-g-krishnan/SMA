# Product Vision

Build SMA as an institutional-grade quantitative trading platform where the supplied PDF corpus is the knowledge core. The platform must support Indian equities, futures, options, ETFs, indices, macro intelligence, technical analysis, fundamental valuation, AI/ML research, backtesting, portfolio construction, risk, automated execution, and broker-safe operations.

The product is not a retail bot. It is a research-to-execution platform with traceability from every indicator, pattern, strategy, risk rule, and model back to the reference documents, OCR addenda, and visual ingestion audits.
