# 18 Portfolio Architecture

## Responsibilities

Holdings, positions, cash, allocation, rebalancing, benchmark comparison, sector exposure, factor exposure, risk contribution, performance attribution, drawdown tracking, and reporting.

## Portfolio Construction

Supports rule-based allocation, factor allocation, sector rotation, volatility targeting, risk parity readiness, cash allocation, and benchmark-relative analysis.

## Data Inputs

Broker holdings, fills, prices, corporate actions, fundamentals, valuation features, risk metrics, strategy signals, and market regimes.

## Outputs

Portfolio dashboard, allocation recommendations, rebalance plans, exposure alerts, risk reports, and performance analytics.
